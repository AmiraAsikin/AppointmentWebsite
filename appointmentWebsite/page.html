our services

Denture

Dentures are removable appliances used to replace missing teeth and surrounding tissue.

(Price Range : RM 200 - RM 1000)

Teeth Whitening

Teeth whitening treatment is an effective way to lighten the original color of teeth without cutting them.

(Price Range : RM 500)

Scaling and Polishing

Dental scaling is a treatment to clean tartar. This treatment needs to be done regularly to prevent tooth and gum damage.

(Price Range : RM 70 - RM 150 )

Tooth Filling

Dental fillings are a treatment to restore a cavity by sealing the space where bacteria can enter.

(Price Range : RM 70 - RM 150)

Root Canal Treatment

Root canal treatment is an alternative treatment for severely damaged teeth where the pulp tissue has become infected or has died, so that the tooth is not removed and can continue to function in the mouth.

(Price Range : RM 400 - RM 1200)

Braces (Orthodontic)

Braces are a tool to correct and straighten the teeth and fix the bite position of the teeth in a jaw.

(Price Range : RM 5000 - RM 7500)

Tooth Extraction

Tooth extraction is a procedure used when the tooth has been damaged and cannot be saved anymore.

(Price Range : RM 70 - RM 150)

Crown and Bridge

A crown is a dental cover used to cover damaged teeth.

(Price Range : RM 400 - RM 700)

Minor Oral Surgery

Minor Oral Surgery is a treatment carried out by an Oral Surgeon or dentist who has a special interest in oral surgery and involves surgical treatment for dental problems involving the teeth, jaw, gums, lips or mouth and soft tissues in the oral cavity.

(Price Range : RM 600 - RM 900)

Veneer

A veneer is a little piece of porcelain that is cemented to the tooth. These veneers are viewed as facial cosmetics that may conceal flaws, geuinely correct the teeth's structure, and accentuate gains made without affecting your natural teeth.

(Price Range : RM 250)

Retainer

A teeth retainer is a custom-made device you wear in your mouth over your teeth. It helps keep your teeth in their new, correct position after braces.

(Price Range : RM 150 - RM 300)

Examination & Consultation

A dental consultation and examination is a process used by a dentist to discuss a plan of action for your oral health needs. The purpose of this consultation and exam is to inform the patient on what defines good oral health and the actions they should take.

(Price Range : RM 10 - RM 35 )
